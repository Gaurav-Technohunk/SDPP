Project Summary — Overall Initiative

सनातन धर्मस्थल प्रबन्धन परिषद (SDPP) is dedicated to temple conservation, pujari welfare, Sanskrit education, cultural revival, and community outreach. Your support strengthens sacred spaces and traditions with accountability and impact.

  • Temple Restoration: conserve shikhara, sanctum, and heritage motifs with traditional methods
  • Pujari Training: structured modules on nitya–karmas, chant, hygiene, and seva–dharma
  • Sanskrit Education: weekend classes, recitation melas, and teacher upskilling
  • Community Outreach: annadanam, heritage counselling, and youth/women initiatives
  • Cultural Revival: artisan support, music–dance events, and festival celebrations

Near–term focus: priority temple repairs, expanding training cohorts, and scaling outreach with full transparency.

“लोकाः समस्ताः सुखिनो भवन्तु ।” — May our work bring peace and happiness to all beings.

Thank you for supporting Dharma: protecting temples, empowering priests, and nurturing culture for future generations.
