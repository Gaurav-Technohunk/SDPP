Ancient Shiva Temple Restoration Ceremony

Witness the re-consecration of the sanctum following meticulous stone cleaning, shikhara repair, and vastu-aligned sanctification. The temple resumes daily nitya-seva with a trained pujari committee and transparent management.

Join devotees, artisans, and scholars as we revive sacred architecture and rituals rooted in the Agamas and Vedic traditions. Families are invited to participate in annadanam and Sanskrit shloka recitations.

Event Highlights

  • Prāṇa-pratiṣṭhā and special Rudrābhiṣekam
  • Traditional stone restoration and ornamentation
  • Pujari Training & code of conduct certification
  • Transparent donation board & audit process
  • Sanskrit recitation and cultural performances
