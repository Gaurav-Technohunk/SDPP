Our Sacred Mission

हर मंदिर में व्यवस्था, हर व्यवस्था में सनातन दृष्टि

भारत की आत्मा उसके मन्दिरों में बसती है। ये केवल ईंट-पत्थर से बने भवन नहीं, बल्कि करोड़ों लोगों की आस्था, संस्कार और सनातन चेतना के जीवंत प्रतीक हैं। किंतु दुख की बात यह है कि अनेक मन्दिर आज भी संगठन, व्यवस्था और दिशा के अभाव में अपने वास्तविक उद्देश्य से भटक रहे हैं। यही वह पृष्ठभूमि है, जिसने “सनातन धर्मस्थल प्रबन्धन परिषद” जैसे प्रयास को जन्म दिया है। एक ऐसा संगठन, जिसका लक्ष्य है “हर मन्दिर में व्यवस्था, हर व्यवस्था में सनातन दृष्टि”।

Divine Wisdom

Inspiring Sanskrit Slokas

Discover the timeless wisdom of Sanatana Dharma through sacred Sanskrit verses

सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः
"May all beings be happy"

This universal prayer from the Upanishads teaches us to wish happiness for all living beings, embodying the essence of compassion and universal love.

अहिंसा परमो धर्मः
"Non-violence is the highest duty"

This profound teaching emphasizes that the highest form of righteousness is to cause no harm to any living being, promoting peace and harmony.

वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम्
"The world is one family"

This ancient wisdom teaches us that all humanity is interconnected, promoting unity, brotherhood, and global harmony.

सत्यमेव जयते
"Truth alone triumphs"

This powerful declaration reminds us that truth is the ultimate victor, encouraging us to always stand for righteousness and honesty.

कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते
"You have the right to perform your duty"

From the Bhagavad Gita, this teaches us to focus on our actions and duties without attachment to results, promoting selfless service.

लोकाः समस्ताः सुखिनो भवन्तु
"May all people be happy"

A beautiful blessing that expresses our wish for universal happiness and well-being for all humanity.

Why Devoted Volunteers Are Vital for Temple Restoration

वित्तेन रक्ष्यते धर्मो विद्या योगेन रक्ष्यते।
मृदुना रक्ष्यते भूपः सत्स्त्रिया रक्ष्यते गृहम्॥

"Dharma is protected by wealth, knowledge by practice, rulers by gentleness, and home by virtuous woman" - Chanakya Niti

Your seva preserves ancient temples, strengthens pujari support, and revives Sanatana Dharma through education, rituals, and community outreach. Together we safeguard our divine heritage.

Spiritual Fulfillment & Divine Seva

Offer your time and skills as seva to the Devas. Experience inner peace while supporting daily worship, cleanliness of temple premises, festival preparations, and helping devotees—service done with bhakti becomes a blessing for all.

Preserve Ancient Temples & Culture

Assist in cleaning and restoring sacred structures, documenting inscriptions, supporting pujari committees, and ensuring traditional rituals are maintained with dignity—help keep our living heritage alive.

Serve Community & Grow Together

Join a warm community of devotees. Contribute to Sanskrit education, youth outreach, annadanam drives, and cultural events—develop leadership and organisational skills while serving a higher purpose.
