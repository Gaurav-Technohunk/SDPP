Conservation & Sacred Renewal

Meticulous restoration of heritage temples including shikhara repairs, sanctum (garbhagriha) rejuvenation, mandapam conservation, and motif preservation under sthapati guidance—respecting traditional methods and sacred geometry.

Scope of Work

Stone cleaning and consolidation, lime plaster and ornament repair, stabilizing structural elements, and reinstating nitya-seva with transparent management and audits.

Play Video

We combine artisan expertise with community participation to ensure durable, authentic outcomes. Donor transparency is maintained through clear milestones and reporting.

Key Deliverables

Authentic conservation, ritual readiness, and sustainable maintenance plans.

  • Sanctum and mandapam conservation
  • Ornamental motif repair and finishing
  • Seva schedule and pujari committee setup
  • Transparent donor board and audits
