Temple Seva & Community Workshop

Hands-on training for volunteers on annadanam logistics, cleanliness drives, devotee guidance, and cultural program support at local mandirs.

Participants will learn seva roles, safety and hygiene norms, and how to coordinate with temple committees to serve devotees effectively and compassionately.

Workshop Outline

  • Annadanam planning and queue management
  • Cleanliness, waste handling, and safety
  • Volunteer rota and communication
  • Special puja days and festival support
  • Community outreach and feedback
